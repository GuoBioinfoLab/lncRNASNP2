We identified 315,234 TCGA cancer mutations on lncRNAs, and the effect of mutation on lncRNA functions was calculated by FATHMM algorithm. We also collected lncRNA expressions in 20 cancers from TANRIC, these cancers are BLCA, BRCA, CESC, COAD, GBM, HNSC, KICH, KIRC, KIRP, LGG, LUAD, LIHC, LUSC, OV, PRAD, READ, SKCM, STAD, THCA, UCEC. The abbreviations of cancers was collected from http://www.bio-info-trainee.com/.
