Welcome to the lncRNASNP2 database
Long non-coding RNAs (lncRNAs) are emerging as key factors in the regulation of various cellular processes and diseases. LncRNASNP is a database providing comprehensive resources of single nucleotide polymorphisms (SNPs) in human/mouse lncRNAs. It contains SNPs in lncRNAs, SNP effects on lncRNA structure, mutation in lncRNAs and lncRNA:miRNA binding.

In lncRNASNP2, numbers of human lncRNAs and SNPs on them were updated to 141,353 and 10,205,295. Furthermore, we identified 859,534 Cosmic Noncoding Variations and 315,234 TCGA cancer mutations based on GRCh38 in these lncRNAs.
In lncRNASNP2, you can browse or search

  • SNPs in lncRNAs and their impacts on lncRNA;

  • miRNA binding in lncRNAs & SNP effects on the bindings;

  • SNPs in GWAS linkage disequilibrium (LD) regions;

  • Mutations on lncRNAs in TCGA cancers

  • lncRNA expression in TCGA cancer data

  • Cosmic Noncoding Variationd(CosmicNCV) in lncRNAs

  • Mutation effects on lncRNAs
